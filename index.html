The Lesson Engine

A tested method behind every ninety-minute session.

Evidence-based pedagogy — retrieval practice, spacing, interleaving, worked-to-faded examples. Published in the cognitive science. Proven in the classroom.

Read the full engine
Segment A0 · 15 min

Diagnostic warm-up

Mixed, spaced retrieval questions from the last 2 – 4 weeks. Silent work, fast mark, circle every sub-topic with ≥ 2 errors.

Segment B15 · 45 min

Worked → faded → independent

Scaffold rigorously. Fade deliberately. The student moves from watching to driving within thirty minutes.

Segment C45 · 75 min

Exam-conditions performance set

15-mark mini-set, blueprint-weighted to match the real paper. Pace guide ~1 mark per minute. Mark and log.

Segment D75 · 90 min

Error log · micro-plan · homework

Classify every miss, write the if/then fix, assign targeted homework in-session. Nothing left to memory.
