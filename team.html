Founder Profile

Isheanesu Makhuza

Founder & Lead Educational Consultant

A UCT-trained Mechanical & Mechatronic Engineer who found his true calling at the intersection of engineering and education.

Isheanesu graduated from the University of Cape Town and went on to build a career in the superyacht and marine manufacturing industry, contributing to globally award-winning projects in the South African marine sector. His deep technical expertise, combined with a genuine passion for mentoring young people, led him to establish Makhuza Educational Consultants in 2020.

Ishe's teaching philosophy is straightforward: don't give students answers — give them the understanding to find answers themselves. That approach has produced consistently exceptional student results and a 4.9/5 star reputation built entirely on word-of-mouth referrals.
