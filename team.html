No. 05 · Leadership

The people
behind the standard.

MEC is run by practising engineers who also happen to love teaching. These are the people who design the curriculum, train the tutors, and uphold the standard every single week.

Leadership 3 founding members
Backed by 12 tutors
All qualified engineers
§ 01
Founder & Principal Tutor

Isheanesu Makhuza, Pr.Eng

Isheanesu is a Zimbabwean-born Mechanical Design Engineer based in Cape Town. He holds a B.Eng in Mechanical Engineering and is a registered Professional Engineer with the Engineering Council. His day-job is in the marine industry, designing rotating machinery and HVAC systems for ships; he is also founder of Tyrell Technologies, a premium mechanical HVAC design consultancy operating across Southern Africa and the Middle East.

His teaching style — confident, specific, first-principles — is a direct reflection of how he works as a design engineer. He treats a student's matric exam the way he treats a pressure-vessel calculation: with a method, a paper trail, and a clear audit of what was covered and what's still uncertain. He still personally tutors a small cohort of matric-to-university students each term to keep his teaching sharp.

Isheanesu founded MEC after watching too many bright young people underperform in STEM — not because the work was beyond them, but because they had been taught by people who had never been taught how to teach. The Tutor Protocol that governs MEC today is the standard he wishes had been applied to his own secondary education.

§ 03 · How We Work

A small firm,
deliberately.

We're building MEC to stay small enough that every client knows the leadership personally — and that the people who answer your WhatsApp are the same people who designed the curriculum.

i.

No account managers

When you WhatsApp MEC, you're speaking to one of the three leadership — or your assigned tutor. That is the whole hierarchy.

ii.

Capped intake

We cap client load per tutor to protect the quality of preparation. A full roster means a waiting list, not a downgrade.

iii.

Engineers first

Every leadership hire is also a tutor. Nobody runs MEC who hasn't recently taught a real student on a Tuesday evening.

iv.

Documented everything

The Protocol. The question bank. The progress reports. The standard isn't in our heads — it is written down, audited, improved.

Work with us

Meet the tutor who will actually teach your child.

On the diagnostic call we match you to a specific tutor — not a pool, not a platform. You'll know their name, their background, and their schedule before you commit.
